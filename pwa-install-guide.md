# 📱 PWA Installation Guide for ChatBox

## 🚀 **Install ChatBox as Mobile App (Easiest Method)**

### **Step 1: Open on Mobile**
1. **Unlock your Android phone**
2. **Open Chrome browser** (important: must be Chrome)
3. **Type or click**: `https://gtramesh.github.io/chatbox`
4. **Wait for page to load**

### **Step 2: Install as App**
1. **Tap the menu button** (⋮) in Chrome's top-right corner
2. **Scroll down** and tap **"Add to Home screen"**
3. **Verify app name**: "ChatBox - Mobile Chat Application"
4. **Tap "Add"** or "Install"
5. **App icon appears** on your home screen

### **Step 3: Use the App**
1. **Tap the ChatBox icon** on your home screen
2. **App opens in full-screen** (no browser bars)
3. **Register or login** with your account
4. **Start chatting** with other users!
